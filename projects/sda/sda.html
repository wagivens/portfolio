Snap Design Academy

The Snap Design Academy is a 9-week design internship at Snap Inc. Academy scholars get to use and further refine skills in a variety of design disciplines such as graphic design and user interface design. Throughout the program, designers are challenged to practice making highly intentional, research-informed design decisions. Much of this is done within the context of Snap's core product, Snapchat. I was a member of the Snap Design Academy's sixth cohort in Summer 2023.

Final Showcase

Final Showcase is a two-week sprint at the end of the Academies wherein scholars work in cross-functional teams (designers, marketers, and developers) to create Snapchat features aimed at remedying various societal issues. My team was tasked with addressing the inaccessibility of higher education for low-socioeconomic populations, as well as the lack of resources first-generation students face leading up to, and throughout, college.

Our research helped us discover low-income and first-generation students are much more likely to drop out of school. Without access to mentorship, tutoring/test prep, and the materials they need when applying to universities, they often find it difficult to navigate the college process and higher education environments.

We decided our solutions should focus on giving under-resourced students the tools they might need (mentorship, direction, accountability frameworks, etc) to build a structure that would help them in their academic careers.

One of our features, Achieve AI, is an offshoot of Snapchat's My AI and is meant to give under-resourced students targeted, individual guidance in the form of an AI mentor. Our other feature, Pods, is meant to replicate in-person group dynamics and foster the increase in accountability/commitment people experience when working with others.

Achieve AI helps students who might not have all the knowledge or motivation they need when navigating the college application process, or once they've actually gotten to a higher education environment. Snapchatters can use Achieve AI to do things like build a study schedule, set pomodoro timers for themselves, or jump into a pod to study with their friends.

Using Achieve AI to create a study schedule.

We envisioned Pods as a way to replicate in-person study groups on Snapchat. With our research, we learned people are generally more likely to keep their commitment to something if they're doing it with others (ex: studying, exercising, learning a language, etc), and are more successful; this would be a great way to help first-gen students lean on each other to remain accountable in environments they are unfamiliar with.

Setting a Pomodoro Timer while in a Pod.

Of course, we made sure not to lose focus of Snapchat's primary vehicle for communication: the camera. Pods would utilize all of Snapchat's exhaustive text and chat features, but would also incorporate video chatting for (virtual) face-to-face interactions.

Switching between chat and video while in a Pod.

People often rely on incentives for motivation, so we integrated one of Snapchat's most popular features, streaks, into our own in the form of Study Streaks; as people study with their friends in pods, they build a study streak for everyone in their group.

The project sprint culminated in the actual showcase, during which every group presented their features to an audience of family, friends, and Snap employees (including Snap executives at the VP and SVP levels).
