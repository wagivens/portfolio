    Streamlining the recycling process for young adults.

    Team: 5 Designers

    My Role: User Research, Flows and Scenarios, Sketching, Wireframing, Information Architecture/Sitemapping, Contributing to/Maintaining Design System, Prototyping, Testing

    Project Summary

    This was a semester-long project encompassing all steps of the Design Thinking framework/process.

    The Challenge

    Young adults are often confused about which products to recycle and how to recycle them due to the wide range of recyclable materials and recycling requirements. As such, recyclable waste often ends up in landfills instead of recycling centers.
